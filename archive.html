Accommodations

Nearby accommodations are limited so please book soon! We have reserved lots of cabins in the area, and they are all pretty unique so we've put them into categories for you below!

We will have shuttles and carpools from the reserved cabins and Comfort Suites only. If you book elsewhere, please arrange transport other than Uber/Lyft because we're in the boonies!

Let us know if you're willing to share a two bedroom cabin with another friend or group who's attending the wedding!

Alpen Way Chalet Mountain Lodge

$185-195/night

Available starting Saturday and allows 1 night stay:

  • Carefree (queen bed + twin)
  • Farmhouse (queen bed)
  • Gracious (king bed)
  • Enchanted (queen bed)
$200-285/night

Available starting Saturday and requires a 2 night minimum stay:

  • Jubilee (two queen beds + queen sofa bed)
  • Affinity (queen bed + sofa bed + 2 twin murphy beds)
  • Bountiful (two queen beds + queen sofa bed)
  • Daisy (king bed + queen sofa bed)
Contact the Lodge (303-674-7467) to book!

Stay at Our House

We will be treating ourselves to a fancy cabin, so if you are looking for a free place to stay with feline companionship and a chance to meet other guests, please stay at our house in Kittredge, or camp on the property! We have 4 double beds and our chaos canines will be staying elsewhere.

Stay at our Friend's House

We have a wonderful and generous family friend who lives in Genesee, CO (just one exit down I-70 from Evergreen and the Comfort Suites) and has two guest bedrooms and two very friendly labradors. Big thank you to our friend!
