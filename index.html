Transportation

We will have shuttles to the big event from the hotels and cabins above! Please plan on taking the shuttle if you're staying at the Comfort Suites, Alpen Way, Highland Haven, or Country Cabins.

There is very limited parking at the venue! We encourage our Denver locals to carpool or park at the Comfort Suites (29300 U.S. Hwy 40, Evergreen, CO 80439) and ride the shuttle too. If you want to drive directly to the venue and park, plan on arriving early as there is no walkable backup lot!

Uber/Lyft aren't going to be super reliable near the venue and accommodations! We are in a rural area and we might have spotty reception at the venue and very few carshare drivers nearby.

Please let us know if you want to be added to a carpooling WhatsApp group for the weekend. We'd love to save guests the expense of booking a rental car. So if you'll need rides or if you're willing to share your ride with others, Email anneanddan25@gmail.com and we will connect you all.

Don't trust Google Maps directions to Dunafon Castle! The turn off to the wedding venue is a few hundred feet from where it is on Google Maps it's a frequently missed turn. Please keep an eye out for the sign if you're driving in.

We Love Each Other

Our Story

We can't believe it's been 6 years together already! We are so thankful for all the great moments along the way, especially those shared with our amazing friends and family.

  • First We Meet

    February 15, 2019

    We met in 2019, as so many couples these days do, through the apps. Dan lured Anne in with a pirate joke, Anne lured Dan in with a cute picture of her not so friendly dog Bear. Dan obliviously suggested a first date on Valentine's Day, and Anne gently suggested the day after instead.

  • The First Year

    2019-The End Times

    Our first year together was marked with real-world travel and adventure: our first weekend trip to romantic Salt Lake City, live music at Red Rocks, hikes and camping around Colorado, Dan's introduction to the Albuquerque Balloon Fiesta, Thanksgiving in New York, and a getaway to Hawaii mere days before Covid lockdown...

  • Quarantine Together

    2020-2022?

    The following year(s) quarantined together were make-or-break for our relationship, settling into our first house with our growing four-legged family. Luckily, no puny coronavirus could break us, and we found fun in the little things in life: weekend hikes and camping trips, experimenting with vegan cooking, trying our best at new hobbies from biking to woodworking to urban gardening, and fostering dogs (sometimes "successfully" and other times failing in the best way possible: yay, Ullie!).

  • Travelin' Times

    2022-2024

    As the world opened up again, our adventures did too, most notably with two trips to Kenya, cross-country road trips to each coast in the "Chaos Caboose," and lots of wedding celebrations for friends and family. Dan and Anne both went through job transitions, Anne's cat Strudel gradually decided that she was Dan's cat, and Ullie and Riley continued to welcome/tolerate sharing their pawrents with foster and boarding pups.

  • We're Engaged!

    August 4, 2023

    Despite becoming quite the fan of true crime and mysteries in her free time, Anne was easily kidnapped and fooled by Dan's engagement coverup plan. He said that we were visiting some friends in Lakewood, but when we were 20+ miles past Lakewood in the valley of Lair o' The Bear park, it became apparent that Dan had a special question to ask. The answer was obviously yes!

  • Mountain Moves!

    August 10, 2024-Forever

    After pet sitting in the mountains one too many times, we started dreaming of living farther out of town with enough space to have a "dog ranch" and beauty to spend the rest of our lives. After lots of waiting and shopping for the right place, we made the move to Kittredge, CO. Just 5 minutes from where we're getting married and 8 minutes from where we got engaged! We are ecstatic and beyond grateful to call this our forever home.
